Part 1: Grammar Summary (Tóm tắt ngữ pháp)

1. Plural Nouns (Danh từ số nhiều)

In English, most countable nouns have plural forms.

🔹 Add 's':

book → books, pen → pens, dog → dogs

🔹 Add 'es':

box → boxes, bus → buses, dish → dishes

2. Adding 's'/'es' (Cách thêm 's'/'es')

  • Add 's': most nouns (cat → cats)
  • Add 'es': nouns ending in s, sh, ch, x, z, o (bus → buses)
  • y → i + es: consonant + y (baby → babies)
  • f/fe → ves: some nouns ending in f/fe (knife → knives)

3. Irregular Nouns (Danh từ bất quy tắc)

These nouns don't follow standard plural rules

child → children

person → people

foot → feet

sheep → sheep

mouse → mice

tooth → teeth

4. Countable/Uncountable (Đếm được/Không đếm được)

Countable:

  • Have plural forms
  • Use with a/an
  • (a book, three apples)

Uncountable:

  • No plural form
  • No a/an
  • (milk, information)

5. Uncountable Categories (Danh từ không đếm được)

These categories are usually uncountable:

  • Materials/liquids: water, rice, air
  • Abstract concepts: love, information, advice
  • Activities/states: swimming, sleep, happiness

6. Noun Positions (Vị trí danh từ)

  • Subject: Students study hard.
  • Object: I love animals.
  • After preposition: Put it on the table.

💡 Tip:

For irregular nouns, you need to memorize them as there are no standard rules. (Các danh từ không theo quy tắc cần học thuộc vì không có quy tắc chung.)
