Grammar Summary

Present Simple (am/is/are)

  • I am a student.
  • He/She/It is at home.
  • You/We/They are happy.
  • Used for present time and permanent states

Past Simple (was/were)

  • I/He/She/It was at school.
  • You/We/They were friends.
  • Used for completed actions in the past
  • Công thức:
  • (+) S + was/were + ...
  • (-) S + was/were + not + ...
  • (?) Was/Were + S + ...?

Negative Forms

  • Add not after the verb
  • I am not tired. / She was not (wasn't) there.
  • They are not (aren't) students.

Questions

  • Was / Were + S + ...?
  • Was she your teacher? → Yes, she was. / No, she wasn't.
  • Were they at school? → Yes, they were. / No, they weren't.
