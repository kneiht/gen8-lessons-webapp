Part 1: Grammar Summary (Tóm tắt ngữ pháp)

1. Usage (Cách sử dụng)

  • Habits and routines (thói quen và việc thường làm)
  • Facts (sự thật hiển nhiên)
  • Permanent situations (tình huống cố định lâu dài)
  • General truths (chân lý phổ biến)

2. Structure (Cấu trúc)

With 'to be' (Với động từ to be)

(+) S + am/is/are + ...

(-) S + am/is/are + not + ...

(?) Am/Is/Are + S + ...?

With normal verbs (Với động từ thường)

(+) S + V(s/es) + ...

(-) S + do/does + not + V + ...

(?) Do/Does + S + V + ...?

3. Signal Words (Dấu hiệu nhận biết)

  • Always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never
  • Every day/week/month/year
  • On Mondays, at weekends
  • Once/twice a week, three times a month

4. Spelling Rules (Quy tắc thêm s/es)

Most verbs: add -s work → works
Verbs ending in -ch, -sh, -ss, -x, -o: add -es watch → watches
Verbs ending in consonant + y: change y to i + es study → studies
Verbs ending in vowel + y: add -s play → plays

Part 3: Basic Exercises (Bài tập cơ bản)

Exercise 1: Fill in the blanks with the correct form of verbs

👉 Complete the sentences with the present simple form of the verbs in brackets:

My sister _______________ (go) jogging every morning.
The sun _______________ (set) in the west.
These insects _______________ (belong to) the same family.
She _______________ (not take on) too much responsibility at work.
________ your brother ________ (do) yoga regularly?

Exercise 2: Choose the correct answer

My hobby ________ making models from cardboard.
Horse riding ________ very popular among teenagers.
My little sister ________ her dollhouse every day.
Gardening ________ me feel relaxed.
________ these bugs belong to the same species?
