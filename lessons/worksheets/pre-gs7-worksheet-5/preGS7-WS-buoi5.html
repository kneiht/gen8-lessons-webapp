Part 1: Grammar Summary (Tóm tắt ngữ pháp)

1. Possessive Adjectives (Tính từ sở hữu)

Used before nouns to show possession

my my book
your your dollhouse
his/her his gardening tools
our our responsibility
their their maturity

2. Possessive Nouns (Danh từ sở hữu)

Formed by adding 's to singular nouns and ' to plural nouns ending in s

  • Singular: John's horse riding equipment
  • Plural: students' yoga mats
  • Irregular: children's cardboard toys

3. Possessive Pronouns (Đại từ sở hữu)

Replace nouns to show ownership

mine This glue is mine.
yours The benefit is yours.
his/hers The insect collection is hers.
theirs The unusual hobby is theirs.

4. Special Cases

  • Joint possession: Tom and Jerry's jogging routine
  • Separate possession: Tom's and Jerry's yoga mats
  • Belong to: This valuable set belongs to my mother.

Part 3: Basic Exercises (Bài tập cơ bản)

Exercise 1: Choose the correct possessive form

👉 Select the correct possessive form for each sentence:

This is ________ dollhouse. (Mary)
These are ________ gardening tools. (children)
That is ________ responsibility. (everyone)
These are ________ yoga mats. (teachers)
This cardboard belongs to ________. (I)

👉 Use: my, your, his, her, our, their

The students take ________ responsibility seriously.
I enjoy ________ jogging routine every morning.
Tom takes ________ horse riding lessons on weekends.
